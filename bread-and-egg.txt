Ingredients:
 - 1 egg
 - Powdered milk
 - Powdered Milo
 - Water
 - Pepper
 - Onions

Directions:
 1. Place oil on a frying pan.
 
 2. After 1 minute, pour blended pepper and onions into it
 
 3. After 30 seconds, pour the egg into it.

 3. When fried enough. Take down

 4. Boil water and make your tea with the powdered milk and milo.
